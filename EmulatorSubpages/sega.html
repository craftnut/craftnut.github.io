Sega

Master System/Game Gear

Emulicious is what I've used in the past, very easy to setup.
The Game Gear was literally a handheld Master System, so most Master System emulators can play both.

Genesis

Genesis Plus GX is the best and Genesis emulator, but it's only available on RetroArch. I think you should still give it a shot, but if you strongly prefer standalone emulators,
Kega Fusion is just OK, albeit outdated and no longer the most accurate, but it's useable.

Saturn

I don't know enough about Saturn to recommend one, please read here if you need a Saturn emulator.

Dreamcast

Flycast is the best option, and only one you should consider. It's very easy to use and doesn't need BIOS.

If you want to emulate more obscure Sega systems, I'd suggest researching on the Emulation General Wiki.
